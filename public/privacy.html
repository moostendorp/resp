Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Effective Date: Upon Platform Launch

1. Introduction

Welcome to resp.ph ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website and use our services.

resp.ph serves as a marketplace connecting real estate professionals with educational opportunities including review classes, CPD seminars, and university programs. By using our platform, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you:

  • Sign up for our waitlist or create an account
  • Register for seminars, review classes, or educational programs
  • List your services as an educational provider
  • Contact us for support or inquiries
  • Participate in surveys, feedback forms, or promotions
  • Post reviews or ratings for educational providers

2.1 Personal Information

  • Basic Information: Full name, email address, mobile number, date of birth
  • Professional Information: PRC license number, license type (Broker/Salesperson), CPD compliance status, accreditation numbers
  • Educational Information: Educational background, current enrollment status, areas of interest
  • Account Information: Username, password (encrypted), profile picture
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed through secure third-party providers)

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, search queries
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (for showing relevant local seminars)
  • Cookies and Tracking: Session cookies, preference cookies, analytics cookies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 Service Delivery

  • Process registrations for seminars, review classes, and educational programs
  • Connect learners with appropriate educational providers
  • Facilitate communication between learners and providers
  • Process payments and issue receipts/certificates
  • Track CPD units and compliance status

3.2 Platform Improvement

  • Personalize your experience with relevant course recommendations
  • Analyze platform usage to improve features and user interface
  • Conduct research and analytics on education trends
  • Develop new features based on user needs

3.3 Communication

  • Send booking confirmations and reminders
  • Notify about CPD compliance deadlines
  • Share updates about new courses and providers
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support
  • Send marketing communications (with your consent)

3.4 Legal and Security

  • Comply with legal obligations under Philippine law
  • Verify provider accreditations with PRC
  • Prevent fraud and maintain platform security
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and policies

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

4.1 With Educational Providers

When you register for a course or seminar, we share necessary information with the provider to facilitate your enrollment and attendance.

4.2 Service Providers

  • Payment Processors: PayMaya, GCash, credit card processors
  • Cloud Services: AWS, Google Cloud for data storage
  • Communication Services: Email and SMS providers
  • Analytics: Google Analytics (anonymized data)

4.3 Legal Requirements

  • To comply with court orders, subpoenas, or other legal processes
  • To cooperate with law enforcement agencies
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Access Controls: Role-based access to personal information
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Data Minimization: We only collect necessary information
  • Employee Training: Regular privacy and security training for staff

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services to you
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax records for 5 years)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our agreements
  • Maintain records for PRC compliance verification

Inactive accounts may be deleted after 3 years of inactivity, with prior notice.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from marketing communications
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@resp.ph or through your account settings.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Keep you logged in to your account
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect platform functionality.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 18, we will take steps to delete such information immediately.

10. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than the Philippines, including countries that may not have the same level of data protection. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers in compliance with Philippine data privacy laws.

11. Philippine Data Privacy Act Compliance

We strictly comply with the Philippine Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) and its Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR). As a Personal Information Controller (PIC), we adhere to the following principles:

11.1 General Data Privacy Principles

  • Transparency: We are transparent about our data processing activities
  • Legitimate Purpose: We only process data for legitimate purposes as stated in this policy
  • Proportionality: We only collect data that is necessary and relevant

11.2 Your Rights Under Philippine Law

As a data subject, you have the following rights:

  • Right to be Informed: Know how your data is being processed
  • Right to Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Right to Object: Object to the processing of your personal information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Right to Data Portability: Obtain your data in a structured format
  • Right to File a Complaint: Lodge a complaint with the National Privacy Commission
