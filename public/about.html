A Message from the Founder

Like you, I've experienced the frustrations of navigating professional requirements. As a PRC-licensed Real Estate Broker, Appraiser, and Consultant, I know what it's like to chase CPD units before renewal ‚Äî joining random Viber groups, scrambling to find seminars at the last minute, and digging through email threads just to locate certificates. Every renewal season, it felt like the same headache.

But the challenge isn't only for licensed professionals. Aspiring practitioners preparing for exams often struggle to find the right review classes to feel confident on licensure day. Many practicing agents and unlicensed persons want to transition into licensed roles but don't always know where to start. And providers spend more time on marketing and admin than on actually teaching and supporting their students.

That's why I built this portal.

This platform is designed for four groups:

  • For aspiring professionals ‚Äî to find trusted review classes that prepare you for licensure.
  • For practicing agents and unlicensed persons ‚Äî to explore pathways and programs that help you transition into licensed roles under RESA.
  • For licensed practitioners ‚Äî to track CPD credits, store certificates securely, and get reminders before renewal. No more lost papers or forgotten deadlines.
  • For providers and institutions ‚Äî to reach your audience more easily. Instead of struggling with design tools and endless Facebook boosting, you can generate a seminar page and poster instantly, with your accreditation and CPD units front and center.

Beyond convenience, this portal also offers a clear and lawful pathway for unlicensed practitioners to upgrade into licensed roles under the RESA Law. This is my contribution to uplifting the real estate profession ‚Äî reducing shortcuts, encouraging compliance, and helping raise the overall standards of practice in the Philippines.

The vision is simple: to make professional growth less of a burden, and more of a clear path you can manage with confidence. This isn't just another tech project. It's built by someone who has faced the same frustrations, and who understands the standards and credibility that professionals demand.

I hope this portal becomes a tool that saves you time, reduces stress, and helps you focus on what really matters: advancing in your profession and serving your clients.

Melissa Magpale Oostendorp

PRC Licensed Real Estate Broker, Appraiser & Consultant

Why resp.ph?

The Philippine real estate industry is experiencing unprecedented growth, with increasing demand for qualified professionals. However, the path from aspiring to licensed practitioner remains challenging:

  • Board Exam Preparation: Finding quality review centers and materials is often based on outdated information or limited recommendations
  • CPD Compliance: Licensed brokers and salespersons struggle to find PRC-accredited seminars that fit their schedule and location
  • Career Development: Limited visibility of specialized courses and advanced training opportunities
  • Provider Challenges: Educational institutions lack a dedicated platform to reach their target audience effectively

resp.ph solves these problems by bringing together all stakeholders in one trusted platform.
