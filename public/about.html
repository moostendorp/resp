About resp.ph

Building the future of real estate education in the Philippines

Our Mission

We're building the first dedicated platform for every stage of a real estate career in the Philippines. From aspiring professionals preparing for board exams to licensed practitioners seeking CPD compliance, resp.ph connects you with the education and training you need.

Our platform addresses the fragmented nature of real estate education in the Philippines. Currently, finding accredited CPD seminars, quality review classes, or university programs requires searching through multiple sources, social media groups, and word-of-mouth recommendations. We're changing that by creating a centralized, trustworthy marketplace where education meets opportunity.

Our goal is simple: make quality real estate education accessible, transparent, and easy to find for everyone in the industry.

Why resp.ph?

The Philippine real estate industry is experiencing unprecedented growth, with increasing demand for qualified professionals. However, the path from aspiring to licensed practitioner remains challenging:

  • Board Exam Preparation: Finding quality review centers and materials is often based on outdated information or limited recommendations
  • CPD Compliance: Licensed brokers and salespersons struggle to find PRC-accredited seminars that fit their schedule and location
  • Career Development: Limited visibility of specialized courses and advanced training opportunities
  • Provider Challenges: Educational institutions lack a dedicated platform to reach their target audience effectively

resp.ph solves these problems by bringing together all stakeholders in one trusted platform.
