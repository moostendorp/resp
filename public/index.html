Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is this site?

It's your one-stop platform for professional growth in real estate ‚Äî from review classes for aspiring licensees, to CPD seminars for practicing professionals, and even university programs for further advancement.

Who can use this site?

Everyone in the real estate profession:

  • Aspiring practitioners preparing for licensure exams
  • Licensed brokers, appraisers, consultants, and salespersons needing CPD compliance
  • Professionals pursuing advanced degrees or specialized training

What makes this different?

Instead of searching in many places, you'll find everything here:

  • Review classes for board exams
  • PRC-accredited CPD seminars
  • University and advanced programs

Plus, you can track your CPD credits and get reminders before renewal deadlines.

Do I need an account?

Yes. With a free account you can:

  • Save your seminar history
  • Track CPD progress
  • Receive compliance reminders

Are the seminars accredited?

Yes. All CPD seminars are listed only from PRC-accredited providers. We ensure all seminars are legitimate and meet professional standards.

How much does it cost?

Creating an account is free.

For practitioners: You only pay the seminar or class fees charged by providers. There's no extra cost to use the platform.

For providers: Joining and listing seminars is free during the pre-MVP stage. Future versions may introduce subscriptions or commission-based pricing for added features. Early adopters will lock in better terms.

I'm a provider. Can I list my programs here?

Yes. If you are a PRC-accredited CPD provider or an institution offering review classes or degree programs, you can apply to be listed. Once approved, you'll be able to publish your seminars or courses on the platform and connect directly with real estate professionals.

What if I have more questions?

You can include questions in the signup form or contact us at support@resp.ph
