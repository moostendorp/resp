Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as a provider?

To register as a provider, click the "Become a Provider" button on our homepage or contact us at providers@resp.ph with your PRC accreditation details. We'll guide you through the verification and onboarding process.

Is resp.ph affiliated with PRC?

No, resp.ph is an independent platform that connects learners with PRC-accredited providers. We verify all provider accreditations but are not directly affiliated with the Professional Regulation Commission.

When will the platform launch?

We're currently in pre-launch phase and expect to go live in Q2 2024. Join our waitlist to be notified as soon as we launch and receive exclusive early-bird offers.

What regions will you cover?

We're launching with providers in Metro Manila, Cebu, and Davao, with plans to expand nationwide by the end of 2024. Our goal is to serve all regions of the Philippines.

How can I partner with resp.ph?

We welcome partnerships with educational institutions, review centers, universities, and industry organizations. Contact our partnerships team at providers@resp.ph to discuss collaboration opportunities.
