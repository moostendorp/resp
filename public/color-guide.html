Usage Guidelines

Key Improvements Made:

  • Hero Section: Changed from gray text to white text on blue backgrounds
  • Body Text: Upgraded from #6B7280 to #4B5563 for better readability
  • CTA Buttons: Brightened green from #059669 to #10B981 while maintaining contrast
  • Footer Links: Changed to white/off-white on dark background
  • Form Focus: Added blue focus ring instead of green for consistency
  • Developer Message: Light amber background with amber border for better visibility

Text Color Rules:

  • On white/light backgrounds: Use #111827 (headings) and #4B5563 (body)
  • On blue backgrounds: Use #FFFFFF with opacity for hierarchy
  • On dark backgrounds: Use #F9FAFB or #FFFFFF
  • Never use light gray (#6B7280) on colored backgrounds
